Five Most Watched Debates in U.S. History

The tradition of protests that demand progressive change in the United States continues.

1. The first debate between Barack Obama and Mitt Romney garnered 67.2 million views on Oct. 3, 2012.

2. The second debate between Jimmy Carter and then-Gov. Ronald Reagan garnered 80.6 million views on Oct. 28, 1980.

3. The first debate between Donald Trump and Joe Biden garnered 73.1 million views on Sept. 29, 2020.

4. The third debate between Donald Trump and former Secretary of State Hillary Clinton garnered 71.6 million views on Oct. 9, 2016.

5. The second debate between President George H. W. Bush and former Gov. Bill Clinton garnered 69.9 million views on Oct. 15, 1992.
